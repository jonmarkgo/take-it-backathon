Take It Back-athon

A hackathon for kicks and giggles!
Both amateurs and pros most welcome.

O RLY?

YA RLY! The only goal is fun for everybody

Take It Back-athon is a different kind of independent hackathon, returning to the days of coding for fun. People of any gender, race or condition are encouraged to attend, and cooperation is stressed over competition. What you can expect at Take It Back-athon:
  • No crazy T&C's such as owning project IP.
  • No exploitation of participants by attendees who want free labour on their existing apps/ideas.
  • No excessive focus on project 'monetization' and 'investment potential'
  • No judging criteria not being adhered to by judges
  • No demo time killers - demos should be valued and celebrated as the magic part of the event
  • No safe ideas - silliness is rewarded!
  • No absurdly large cash prizes that attract a different crowd and promote less than friendly behaviour

Inclusive

  • Everyone is welcome
  • We have a strict code of conduct
  • No hackathon experience needed

Fair

  • No celebrity or VC judges
  • We follow our own rules
  • Sponsors don't run the show

Fun

  • Hack on anything you want
  • Making things is awesome
  • Goofy prizes
